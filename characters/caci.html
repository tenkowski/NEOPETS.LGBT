CACIWAGUA, BABY ZOMUTT HISSI
VIEW ON: NEOPETS | OPENNEO
Raised by spooky-colored Neopets, Caciwagua can be considered a prodigy in all things "rawr". She's always wearing her Zomutt kigu at home, and always loves to pretend play with her mom and Bori. Imagine coming home to find the sofa cushions in your living room scattered evenly across the floor like a stepping stone path: that's Caci wanting to play a game of "The Floor Is Lava" with you. Slithering up trees just high enough to be above your eye level and get your attention: that's her pretending to be the cat stuck in a tree that you have to save.
