A hinderance and a nuisance to Meridell and even the Darigan Citadel, his homeland. Vim harbors an ill will towards supporters of the Battle for Meridell conflict and especially Lord Darigan. He takes what the zealots of Meridell took from him by destroying and burning what they hold dear. Desipte his short fuse, he's found a place in Tenko's survey group, using his expertise from his service in Kass's army to infiltrate and obstruct the opposition. If you like what Kass believed in, he already likes you.

Interacts with:

Tenko: Even though Tenko is the designated leader of the team, depending on the day it can feel like either him or Tenko has control. They butt heads often, but are never genuinely mad at each other. As Tenko is the best magic user in the team and Vim is the best physical attacker, they sometimes pick on each other's ability to lead the team to the delight or dismay of the others.

Sosa: Respects Sosa's Battledome prowess and regularly spars with them to practice their swordsmanship. Knows Sosa excels in one thing he lacks in, disipline. He never views himself as greater or lower than them and treats them more seriously than Tenko.

Machu: No matter the day, he sees a healer in Machu first then all comes afterwards. Is quick to be put in his place by her when he takes her healing powers for granted. And it works every time. In fact, most of their time together is time spent while they're excused settling matters away from the team...

Name origin:
Vim is a Krawk with both a itchy trigger finger and ability to get shit done. This gives him the nickname of Vim. His color pallete is also similar to the colors of Vim Factory from this game.

Vim's real name (name on Neopets) is Xinvar. Xinvar is a combination of the chinese character 心 xīn (heart) and var as in variable. Vim's heart towards others varies widely.
