HOW TO NAVIGATE
Look for these things in each page:
※ References: Glimpse at what they look like
※ Avatars: Their personality, described in Neoboard avatars
※ Playlist: A song or two or more relatable to them
※ Lore: Stories and more to learn about them
※ Art: Extra art goes near the bottom of pages
※Art Credit: Artists are always credited here!
※ : New! See the Neopets star next to a link? That means it'll take you to a page on Neopets.com (you likely need to be signed in)
