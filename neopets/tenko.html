CHIBI ANTHRO FORM BY SIXOFHEARTZ
PREFERRED FORM
HEIGHT: 140cm
BUILD: SQUISHY
BODY: TRANSMASC

This is Tenko's form of choice. They are most comfortable with this body. This form is the end result of a collaboration between Edna the Witch and the Lab Ray Scientist.

Tenko had always wished a smaller, cuter anthro form. Their Full Anthro Form was strong indeed, but a Chibi Form would allow Tenko to sacrifice their strength in exchange for higher intelligence. It is this form that allows Tenko to be what they always wanted to be, an all-powerful Darigan Bori Mage -- and they knew the right Neopets to ask about this new form.

Edna and the Scientist were interested in this Chibi Anthro Project, and together they worked tirelessly on this project. Tenko would learn about Edna's transformation magic, and the Scientist would "blueprint" this new body of Tenko, as well as lend the Lab Ray to Edna and Tenko for this project.

For their safety, Tenko's first transformation would be guided by Edna and the Scientist. The Scientist dimmed the Laboratory's lights, drew all power to the Lab Ray, and zapped Tenko in a dazzling beam of light, while Edna cocooned Tenko in a veil of magic to protect them from harm.

With starstuck eyes, Tenko emerged from the cloud of smoke -- transformed and unharmed -- all according to plan. And while Edna and the Scientist did their part, the unpredictable Lab Ray had changed something else about this form.

You see, there's an approx. 8% chance that a Lab Ray zap will change a Neopet's gender. Surely, the Scientist did not plan for this outcome to happen. And while this outcome could easily be reverted, no, it wasn't needed. This would be Tenko's favorite form, and in this form, Tenko is transmasc.

Whenever Tenko revisits this form, their form will look akin to the form they donned while at the Laboratory. Tenko could -- if they wanted -- modify their form even further, but naw. Tenko's pretty happy like this :). They'd rather use their magic to hold onto this form.

"Sustaining this form requires much upkeep from Tenko", Edna noted. Tenko must regularly conjure spells they've learned from Edna on themself to keep their form up. Otherwise, they'll rapidly weaken in this form, and if left unchecked, will collapse and need to be revitalized by Edna.

In case of emergency, the Rod of Dark Nova Tenko carries around can be used to conjure many spells that Tenko can usually do on their own, including transformation spells. It is a very versatile tool, however it mustn't get into the wrong hands.

FERAL FORM BY CIRKUS
HEIGHT:40cm
BUILD: BOWWI
BODY: MASC

This is Tenko's true form, an untransformed feral Bori. Lawyerbot requires Tenko to keep this form on Neopets.com! Despite this, everyone knows Tenko best as an anthro.

He is the most nimble Bori in this form, but he claims to only use this form to be pesky and "fuck around and find out" (as if he already doesn't do that in his other forms).

You better watch out for Tenko at 40 cm! He's just as rawr and chompy here as he is when he's at a towering 190 cm!

Whether he's been bruised up badly, being damned by the presence of malicious Wraith Neopets, and prolonged exposure to one can render him stuck as a four-legged friend. This is why Edna the Witch and Tenko's Rod of Dark Nova are his keys to survival on his journey.

FULL ANTHRO FORM BY CIRKUS
HEIGHT: 190cm
BUILD: DIET MUSCLEGUT
BODY: MASC OR TRANSMASC

Before the Chibi Anthro Project and back when Tenko only had a surface-level understanding of magic, they only knew of one transformation: a form that would make them appear as this tall, musclegut-like Bori.

They're so full of vitality! Tenko likes to work out nonstop and show off more in this form. Well, this comes at the cost of losing INT. And just like their Chibi Anthro Form, they can't sustain this form forever, however they can sustain it using far less magic. This appears to be the case because Tenko is intended to stay in this form while anthropomorphized, and challenging that by being chibi takes more of a toll on Tenko's body. It's okay though, you're a Mage, Tenko! Magic's got your back ★

FURSUIT BY DOMINIQUE DE LEON
Based on Tenko's Full Anthro Form

Tenko is real! His magic doesn't work on Earth, and he's stuck in Full Anthro Form.


Tenko's nose is an interesting case! His snout is somewhat dragon-like in feral form. But in either anthro form, you can draw his nose in two ways:

- Normal nose: The usual nose is drawn is how you see it in the anthro reference sheets. The nose is pretty much a little "v" shape at the end of the snout.

- Cartoony nose: Tenko's nose is a darker color, bigger and more bulbous like a canid. Fursuit Tenko and Tenko's 3D model uses this nose type.


The eyes are capable of lighting up a very bright red. Tenko activates them sparringly, usually in dark and high energy situations like the nearest rave.
