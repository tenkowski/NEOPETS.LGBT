The oldest Neopet in Tenko and Co.'s friend circle. Sosa has traveled the six seas of Neopia and felled countless worthy opponents with their Golden Pirate Sword, making them one of, if not the best Draik swordfigher in all of Neopia.

Partners with Qiru, and open with Machu and Tenko. Grew up with Machu on Krawk Island before Machu was forced to move off the island. Now lives with Qiru in a quaint seaside house by the shores of Meridell.

As you can see, Sosa is kinda epic.
★ Sosa was raised as a Pirate Draik by their experienced and well-off pirate parents, who taught them fencing and swordfighting after realizing Sosa loved playing with pool noodles too much.

★ In their teen years they would go on to compete at youth fencing tournaments, wowing every instructor who had the privilege of teaching Sosa. Sosa loved to play around and experiment, and would even sneak into the Battledome to make some pocket change to put their power to the test. Ultimately, they were never caught, and raked in plenty of Neopoints and rewards for their effort. Even Machu, Sosa's favorite classmate, managed to sneak into the Battledome and cheer Sosa on.

★ Post-high school graduation and after turning 18, they were already prepared to move out and into the college life. Now away from their parents prying eyes, they wanted to get more experimental. Using a good chunk of Neopoints they had saved up during their teenage years, they would buy a Grey Paint brush and some new clothes, and then a dress, etc... They felt much more comfortable with their new college look, yet would leave their parents unaware of the changes for the time being.

★ Sosa would still fence and win medals at fencing tournaments as a Grey Pirate Draik. However, they would be taking a break at skirmishing at the Battledome to focus on their first year of college. And while Sosa was learning at college, so were the opponents in the Battledome. After a successful first year, summer rolled around, and Sosa would make their anticiped return to the Battledome, where they were swiftly defeated by the BD's hardened foes. Sosa would try again twice, then thrice to no avail. The game has changed, and Sosa was not ready. Sosa was not used to defeat, and they promptly cut their losses and left the Battledome, injured inside and out.
