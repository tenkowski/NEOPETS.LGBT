Excerpt from Tenko

ศพโอริกซ์, that's the name I gave her. It's Thai for "The Oryx's Corpse". Nearing the end of my grand journey to receive as many blessings as I could from Neopia's leaders, I found this beautfiul Ogrin spirit alone at a secluded shrine that locals had revered and told me about. It was never clear to me whether she was watching over this particular shrine, or if we were both on the move and our meeting was complete happenstance.

Not counting that beast Cococchi who is in a league of her own and not even affiliated with us, Sphoryx is the most odd one out of everyone in my party, but we accept her all the same. She does not live with us, but she knows how to get to us. She's replaced Machu in Y26, as she possesses the restorative powers we need to keep going.
